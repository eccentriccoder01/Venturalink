Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Venturalink collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal information you provide during registration (name, email, etc.).
  • Usage data, such as pages visited and actions taken on the platform.
  • Cookies and similar technologies for analytics and preferences.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our services.
  • To communicate with you about your account and updates.
  • To personalize your experience on Venturalink.

3. Data Protection

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access and disclosure.

4. Sharing of Information

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We may share data with trusted partners for service delivery, under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • We may disclose information if required by law.

5. Your Choices

  • You can update or delete your account information at any time.
  • You may opt out of marketing communications.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

7. Contact Us

For any questions about this policy, contact us at support@venturalink.com.
