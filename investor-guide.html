Investor Onboarding Process

1. Complete Your Investor Profile

To start discovering promising startups, complete your investor profile. This will help us tailor the opportunities we show you.

  • Specify your investment thesis, including your preferred industries, business models, and funding stages.
  • Indicate your typical investment size and what you bring to the table beyond capital (e.g., mentorship, network access).
  • Link to your LinkedIn profile or personal website to provide more context for entrepreneurs.

2. Discover Investment Opportunities

Once your profile is set up, you can start browsing startup proposals on the platform. Use our powerful search and filtering tools to narrow down the options based on your criteria.

3. Evaluate Startups

When you find a startup that interests you, you can view their detailed proposal, including their pitch deck, financial projections, and team information. Our platform provides all the information you need to make an informed decision.

4. Connect with Founders

If you're impressed with a startup, you can connect with the founders directly through our secure messaging system. Ask questions, schedule meetings, and begin the due diligence process.

5. Manage Your Deal Flow

Keep track of all your potential investments in one place. Our dashboard allows you to manage your deal flow, from initial contact to final investment.
